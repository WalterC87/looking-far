Harold Lee Bustle Jr, 51

Chicago, Illinois

[Chicago, Il] It’s a cold crisp afternoon. Outside a coffee house I meet street performer Harold Lee Bustle Jr, 51, who like me is just trying to stay warm.

Harold is somewhat frustrated at modern parenting, he tells me just yesterday he rescued a little girl that had somehow lost her parents in the busy streets. She was crying and Harold managed to put a smile on her face and find her parents.

“I Don’t think that girl would have come to me for help if I hadn’t been wearing my outfit”. “I’ll tell you, there’s nothing like seeing a child smile”.

Harold has been performing and teaching others to perform all over the US for a couple of decades now.
