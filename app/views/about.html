This project seeks to connect people by asking three simple, yet universal questions:

what are your Fears, Aspirations and Regrets?

This Project is a work in progress, a first volume of a body of work that, by cataloguing portraits of people with their responses, age, name and location, aspires to become an artwork which also serves as a reference point for the emotional and psychological human condition. Above all this projects hopes to trigger a fruitful retrospection in both the reader and the subject. As such, this work should be a tool. A tool for a journey of self retrospection through that of others.

I hope you enjoy the journey.
